HINTON MARKET APPLICATION

INSTALLATION & BUILD:
1. Unzip the provided zip file.
2. Open the Qt creator.
3. Open the project by selecting the .pro file.
4. Press the green 'Run' Button located on the bottom right of the screen.

NOTES:
- To log out, simply click on the 'X' button on the top-right
- To exit the program, click on the 'X' button on the top-right when you're on the login page.

- 'View Users' page for Operators allow you to cancel bookings and see other user information.
- 'Market Schedule' page only allows Operators to make a booking for users.
